About the CBW Series

Our workshop instructors are experts in their fields. Along with the CBW Scientific Committee, the core instructors are responsible for the ongoing curriculum development and the management of the workshops. Visit our instructor page for a list of our core instructors, past and present instructors, and invited lecturers.
